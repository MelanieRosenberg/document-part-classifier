Washington and Lee University requires all full-time students to carry health insurance.
You have the option of purchasing a Student Health Insurance Plan offered to all full-time students.

University.

select W&L from the list of schools and click on “Review Brochure” or “Enroll Now.” Eligible
dependents, including domestic partners, may enroll in this insurance plan once the student is
enrolled.
