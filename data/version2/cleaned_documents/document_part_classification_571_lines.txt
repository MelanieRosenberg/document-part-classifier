If you are aggrieved by this order, you have the right to request a hearing before the Board of Health. This request must be made by either the owner of Victory Baptist Church or an authorized agent of Victory Baptist Church in writing, and filed with the Board of Health within seven (7) days after the date this Order was served or actually received. Any affected party has the right to appear at said hearing.
Failure to comply with this order may result in additional penalties as permitted by law, including but not limited to, the filing of a complaint in District or Superior Court and any emergency court order(s) issued pursuant to any such complaint.
